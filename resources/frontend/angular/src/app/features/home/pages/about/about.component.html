The End is The Start - Retromize

and Retromize was BORN!

No, it wasn't that quick. Retromize was first created by Tom Thornton during a time when he was looking for a new career. Retromize was to be his most ambitious project yet. The time it took demonstrated this and despite being out of work and needing the project complete to get employed, he persevered.

The site you see today is built from those foundations and has evolved over time. Retromize has now become a fast growing and upcoming gaming news network with a passionate community, who share their articles while discussing their favorite news and games!

You are free to browse at your own leisure while on the site but the real fun starts when you sign up. You'll get a dedicated profile, accolades based on your contributions but most importantly, you'll be part of a community who share your interests.
